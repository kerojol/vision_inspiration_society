    COMPANY PROFILE

    V.I.S abbreviation of Vision Inspiration Society is an education company focused on providing quality and accessible education to the society. As one of the market leaders, V.I.S is continuously on improving and enhancing our quality of services to ensure our customers are having the best experience.

    Partnership

    We are taking pride of becoming one of the market leaders in certain Asia and Africa countries. V.I.S is also cooperating with few major universities such as MMU, NUS and University of Nairobi to bring impact to the local community on education. V.I.S is also partnership with various NGO to bridge the gap of education between the urban and rural communities.

    Operation

    V.I.S is currently having 1200 employees in 25 operating countries. V.I.S is looking forward to deliver the best learning experience through the latest technology. V.I.S is committed to a long-term investments to ensure our customers to gain the remarkable learning experience. We are faithfully attached to Corporate Responsibility (CR) to ensure the welfare of our workforce are well taken.

    HISTORY

    V.I.S was launched in 2001. In the year 2001, Chan Yun Hong and Jason created an online tuition website that provides education to every online user regardless of their income status. At that moment, the Internet was not popular, so they were having a hard time managing the company. Problems such as high expenses is a challenge for a startup company. In 2005, V.I.S finally discovered their jump point while cooperating with MMU. V.I.S started to cooperate with the National University of Singapore (NUS) and the University of Nairobi in the year 2007. After 3 years, V.I.S had become market leaders in certain Asia and Africa countries. Recently, V.I.S moved to a 69-floor skyscraper with approximately 1200 employees that are experts in their profession and they are distributed in 25 operating countries

    Our future plan is to creating a new platform for individuals who want to apply for online coaching. We will also provide online coaching on Youtube. For instance, users are allowed to choose for a specific tutor on a 1 to 1 online tuition by paying a small amount of money. We plan to cooperate with retailers and sponsors to advertise on our websites to generate revenue. Moreover, we will open to the public for donations to support our company.

    We predict in the upcoming years , we will be able to automate the running of the company with AI, which will cut down our expenses by 45% and increase our income by 75%. This is because a new platform such as Youtube Online Learning will run on efficient algorithms and update by AI feedback. The popularity of our website will keep on snowballing as our current user base will spread our platform to other potential users. In the long run, this will further increase our company’s income by a minimum of 30%.
